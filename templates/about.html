How The App Works
The schedule page is your live week. The app tracks the week in {{ app_timezone }}. When a new Monday starts, the previous week is archived and a fresh week begins. Your carry-forward note stays so you can resume cleanly.
Pages
Schedule: current week only.
Past Weeks: archived weeks after rollover or manual reset.
Compare Last 4: Gemini comparison across your recent archived weeks.
Review Week: wins, misses, and adjustments for the current week.
Edit Weekly Template: changes the recurring system itself.
Main Controls
Mark done: marks a block complete.
Make focus: marks the one block that matters most for that day.
Next Important Thing: your resume note for later.
Day Notes: notes for the selected day only.
Archive this week and start fresh: saves the week into history and clears the live week.
